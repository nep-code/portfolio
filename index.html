About Me

Nep is a passionate designer and innovative developer with meticulous attention to detail for crafting elegant solutions to complex problems. With 14 years of experience in the field, He had the privilege of working on various projects that have honed his skills and allowed him to contribute meaningfully to the tech industry.

His expertise lies in digital ads incorporating interactive elements such as animations, games, quizzes, and user interfaces for different platforms. HTML, CSS, JavaScript – He speaks the languages that make ads come to life while ensuring every element resonates with the audience and aligns with brand identity.

One of his proudest accomplishments was the Alexa-Enabled Space Station project for Amazon, acknowledged by MediaPost - OMMA Awards that won 3 categories in 2019: Artificial Intelligence, Interactive: Single Execution, and UX/UI.

When not working, he enjoys playing competitive online games while intermittently playing badminton to stay in shape and maintain his physical health.
