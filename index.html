About Me

Hello and welcome to my humble portfolio!

My name is Neptali Calonge, a current Senior OLA Developer at Hedy Philippines Inc. seeking any design/development opportunities that will help them streamline their operations, increase their efficiency, and improve their bottom line.

One of my proudest accomplishment was my project Alexa-Enabled Space Station by true[X] for Amazon, which was recognized by MediaPost - OMMA Awards that won 3 categories in 2019: Artificial Intelligence, Interactive: Single Execution and UX/UI.

When I'm not working, I enjoy playing online games that involve players competing against each other. I also intermittently play badminton with my family and friends to stay in shape and maintain my physical health.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me, and I look forward to the opportunity to work with you and create something amazing.
